Pride and Prejudice

By Jane Austene

Category : General

Description

"Pride and Prejudice," the celebrated novel by Jane Austen, was first published in 1894 in a special edition known as the Peacock Edition. This edition, published by George Allen, is renowned for its striking cover design by Hugh Thomson, featuring an elaborate gold peacock motif on a dark green cloth cover. Thomson, a prominent illustrator of the time, also provided over 100 detailed illustrations throughout the book, bringing Austen's characters and settings to life with intricate charm. The Peacock Edition is highly sought after by collectors and Austen enthusiasts for its literary significance and exquisite artistic presentation.
